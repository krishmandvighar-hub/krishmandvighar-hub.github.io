About

I'm an aspiring Data Analyst who enjoys the full path from a messy spreadsheet to a dashboard someone actually trusts.

My work sits across SQL, Python and Power BI — cleaning and structuring raw data with Pandas and NumPy, exploring it with statistics, and visualizing it in a way that makes the story obvious at a glance. I care as much about the "why" behind a number as the number itself.

I've applied this across retail, sports and HR datasets, building dashboards that surface patterns in sales, performance and workforce trends — see a few of them below.
